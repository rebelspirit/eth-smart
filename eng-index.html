x2 or double the contribution

In the Ethereum fund x2invest there is a fixed profit program or equal rights between investors. If a participant likes the project and participation in it, he can start all over again by re-investing x2invest in Ethereum.

The integration of the maximum profit function is established for the long life of the project and is calculated to the smallest detail. Isn't that enough for you?
